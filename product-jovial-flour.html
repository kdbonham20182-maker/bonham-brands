Our Evaluation

Why We Chose Jovial Einkorn Flour

We selected Jovial 100% Organic Einkorn All-Purpose Flour because it represents one of the cleanest flour options available in the United States. Einkorn is an ancient grain — never hybridized, never genetically modified, cultivated essentially unchanged for thousands of years. That matters because modern wheat has been extensively bred and modified for yield and shelf life, often at the expense of digestibility and nutritional integrity.

What gives us further confidence in Jovial is their certification stack. Regenerative Organic Certification goes well beyond USDA Organic — it requires soil health practices, animal welfare standards, and farmer fairness criteria that most organic brands never meet. On top of that, The Detox Project independently lab-tested this specific product and certified it Glyphosate Residue Free at the lowest detectable government-recognized limits. That's not a marketing claim — it's a documented lab result.

Our one note of transparency: like all flour, einkorn carries inherent mold risk during storage if not kept properly. Jovial does not publicly disclose mold testing. Store in a cool, dry place and use within a reasonable timeframe.
