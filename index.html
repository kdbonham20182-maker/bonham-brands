What is Bonham Gold Label?

We Seek the Best.
We Tell You What We Know.

The Bonham Family spent years discovering that "natural," "organic," and "clean" labels often meant very little. Products with premium certifications still tested positive for heavy metals, pesticides, and microplastics. So we started doing the research ourselves.

The Bonham Gold Label is our honest evaluation of the best available product in each category. Sometimes that means published lab results and full Certificates of Analysis. Sometimes it means a trusted third-party certification or the testimony of a reputable organization. We always tell you which — and we never pretend to know more than we do. Our standard is simple: find the best, be honest about it.

⚗️
Heavy Metals
We look for brands that test for and disclose arsenic, lead, cadmium, and mercury levels.
🌿
Pesticides
Beyond organic certification — we seek products with actual residue testing and documented results.
🔬
Microplastics
We prioritize products that test for and disclose microplastic contamination in their supply chain.
🧪
Chemicals & Toxins
We evaluate whether products contain harmful additives, bleaching agents, or industrial chemicals.
🍄
Mold
Where relevant, we assess mold risk and whether brands test or disclose mold contamination.
🌱
Yeast
For applicable products, we evaluate yeast contamination risk and any available testing disclosures.
🧬
GMO
We look for non-GMO verification and assess whether genetic modification is a concern for the product type.
✓
Verified
Every product is evaluated — whether through published lab data, trusted certifiers, or our own research.
🌾
Purity
We value what is natural and whole. Minimal processing, clean sourcing, and honest ingredients.
