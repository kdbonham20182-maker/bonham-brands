Our Evaluation

Why We Chose Frontier Co-op

We chose Frontier Co-op Baking Soda because it is USP Grade #1 — the United States Pharmacopeia's highest commercial purity standard for sodium bicarbonate — sourced from naturally mined nahcolite deposits with no chemical synthesis and no additives of any kind. It is the strongest purity specification available for a consumer baking soda product.

Frontier Co-op has been a member-owned cooperative since 1976. Their commitment to never irradiating products and never treating them with ethylene oxide — a chemical sterilant found as a contaminant in some supplement products — is a documented, standing policy across their entire product line. The USP Grade #1 specification means the raw material itself meets pharmaceutical-grade purity requirements, not just food-safe standards.

We are transparent about what we do not know: no US baking soda brand currently has published independent third-party heavy metals testing with non-detect results from Lead Safe Mama, Mamavation, or a comparable independent program. This category has not been prioritized for independent testing. This is reflected in the Verified score. Within the constraints of what is currently verifiable, Frontier Co-op's USP Grade #1 specification is the strongest quality claim available.

Honest Transparency Note

  • No published independent third-party heavy metals testing results are publicly available for Frontier Co-op Baking Soda specifically
  • The entire US baking soda category has not been independently tested for lead, cadmium, mercury, and arsenic by Lead Safe Mama, Mamavation, or comparable investigators as of May 2026
  • USP Grade #1 means the raw material meets pharmaceutical-grade purity specifications — this is a meaningful standard, but it is not the same as published independent lab results with non-detect results for specific heavy metals
  • Nahcolite deposits in the Piceance Creek Basin of Colorado — the source of naturally mined US sodium bicarbonate — are among the purest natural sodium bicarbonate deposits in the world
