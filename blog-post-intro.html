Who We Are and Why We Started Bonham Brands

The Bonham Family  ·  May 19, 2026

We are a Catholic, health-conscious family that grew tired of being misled by the products we trusted. We read the labels. We paid the premiums. We chose "natural" and "organic" — only to discover months later that many of those products still contained concerning levels of toxins, pesticides, microplastics, and heavy metals.

Our spare time became consumed by research. Rather than back down, we made it our primary focus — and Bonham Brands was born. We are putting our name on every product we discover to be the pinnacle of its category, stamping it as a Bonham Gold Label Product, and sharing what we find with the world.

We want this company to be a force for good.

Our Mission

To identify and share the purest, highest quality, and most natural products available — and to promote them through the Bonham Gold Label Products designation. We put in the time so you don't have to.

Our Values

We operate with honesty and integrity, guided by our Christian values. We evaluate products based on their actual purity and quality, not on financial incentives or affiliate relationships. We will only feature products we believe truly meet the highest standards.

We Are Consumers First

We use these same products in our own home. Our family is on a journey to find and use only the cleanest options available, and we created this brand to make that process easier for others who feel the same way. We also want to build a community. If you discover a product you believe deserves recognition, we welcome you to share it with us.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We are committed to being transparent about our relationships with the brands we feature. We will recommend products we stand behind, even in cases where no affiliate partnership exists.

Thank you for following along as we continue to research and share the Bonham Gold Label Products. Follow us on X and subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
