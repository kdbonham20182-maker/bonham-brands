Our Story

About Us

Who We Are

A Family on a Mission
to Find the Truth.

We are a Catholic, health-conscious family tired of being misled by the products we trusted. We read the labels. We paid the premiums. We chose "natural" and "organic" — only to discover months later that many of those products still contained concerning levels of toxins, pesticides, microplastics, and heavy metals.

Our spare time became consumed by research. Rather than back down, we made it our primary focus — and Bonham Brands was born. We are putting our name on every product we discover to be the pinnacle of its category, stamping it as a Bonham Gold Label Product, and sharing what we find with the world.

We want this company to be a force for good.

Our Standard

The Bonham Gold Label

The Bonham Gold Label is our designation for the best available product in each category. It is not a third-party certification — it is our honest, independent evaluation based on the best information available to us at the time of review.

Sometimes that means a product has published Certificates of Analysis and full lab transparency. Sometimes it means a trusted third-party certifier has verified the product against standards we respect. Sometimes it means the sourcing, farming practices, and ingredient integrity speak for themselves. We always tell you which — and we never pretend to know more than we do.

As Bonham Brands grows, our goal is to build the Gold Label into a recognized and respected standard — and one day, potentially a third-party certification of our own. For now, it is our family's name on the line with every product we feature. That means everything to us.

Total Transparency

We Tell You Everything.

We will display all of our business relationships. We vow to only host the purest, highest quality, and most natural products — not the best paying ones. If a company won't work with us, we will still feature and commend them for having an exceptional product, without bias.

Our goal is to grow this business through affiliate marketing first. We will always disclose when a link is an affiliate link. Companies distributing products with integrity should be endorsed regardless of our financial relationship with them. Their success creates a market incentive for other companies to follow suit — and that benefits everyone.
