Our Evaluation

Why We Chose Nature's Sodium Bicarbonate

Most baking soda sold in the United States is produced using the Solvay process — a synthetic chemical manufacturing method that uses ammonia, salt brine, and carbon dioxide to produce sodium bicarbonate. While the end product is technically pure, the process relies on industrial chemical inputs that many health-conscious consumers would prefer to avoid entirely.

Nature's Sodium Bicarbonate by Healthy Kidney Inc. is different. It is produced using a natural hot water process from naturally occurring U.S. mineral deposits — specifically from trona ore, the naturally occurring mineral form of sodium bicarbonate found primarily in Wyoming. This process requires no synthetic chemicals, no ammonia, and no aluminum compounds whatsoever. The result is the highest USP grade sodium bicarbonate available, produced in an FDA-registered facility under GMP standards, with manufacturing processes that meet OMRI organic use eligibility standards.

We are transparent that this product is verified primarily through brand claims and manufacturing standards rather than published independent lab results. However, the sourcing method, production process, and facility standards are meaningfully cleaner than conventional baking soda alternatives.
